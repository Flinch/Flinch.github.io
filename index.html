Saitama

My biggest gripe when it comes to watching shows or anime, is trying to figure out what to watch next. To remedy this, I am building an application that is able to store a users favorites animes, and then analyze them so as to return recommendations on what to see next. The app features a React front-end and Ruby back-end. Please note that I am currently addressing a DB bug after migrating to a different deployer
